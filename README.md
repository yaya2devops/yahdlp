An innovative tool, you give it;
- an email, 
- a phone, 
- or even both.

As per the inspect phase.

```
npm i -g yahdlp
```

You target an asset of yours, you give it; 
- your email, 
- your phone, 
- or even both.

If it is detected, 
- The asset is completely redacted.
- DLP with a nice color. 

Else, we will tell you.
